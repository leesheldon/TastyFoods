
	Spring roll

Spring Rolls are the vegetarian super light, crispy and tender vegetarian appetizer cousin of the traditional egg roll. Spring Rolls are the perfect dim sum appetizer alongside Crab Rangoon, Shrimp Tempura and Potstickers.

	Samosa

The samosa is made with all-purpose flour locally known as maida shell stuffed with some filling, generally a mixture of mashed boiled potato, onions, green peas, lentils, spices and green chili, or fruits.

	Papdi chat

Papdi Chaat or Papri Chaat is a yummy snack assorted with crunchy base of crispy poori which is topped with lip-smacking chutneys, veggies and curd. ... Made using a motley of bases, chutneys, munchies and fresh ingredients, Chaat can be sweet, tangy, spicy or savoury!

	Malai Kofta

It is made from onion-tomato-cashews. It needs very basic and usual spices. Addition of cashews and malai makes it rich. Kofta are made from grated paneer and boiled, mashed potatoes. Dried fruits are added in kofta, it gives the little crunch in it.

	Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken tikka masala is chicken tikka, chunks of chicken marinated in spices and yogurt, that is then baked in a tandoor oven, and served in a masala (spice mixture) sauce. ... The sauce and chicken pieces may be coloured orange using foodstuffs such as turmeric, paprika, tomato purée or with food dye.

	--------------------------------------------------
	Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer butter masala recipe is made with makhani gravy. the word 'makhani' means buttery and is derived from the word 'makhan' which means butter. makhani gravy is made with butter, tomato puree, cashews or cream and a few herbs & spices.

	--------------------------------------------------
	Jalebi

Jalebi, also known as Zulbia, is a sweet popular in countries of South Asia, West Asia, North Africa, and East Africa. It is made by deep-frying maida flour batter in pretzel or circular shapes, which are then soaked in sugar syrup.

	--------------------------------------------------
	Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a combination of two words “Ras” meaning juice and “Malai” meaning cream. The dessert is also described as a rich cheese cake without a crust. The name itself is exotic and denotes the richness of this delicate Indian sweet.

	--------------------------------------------------
	Mango Lassi

Mango Lassi is made from yogurt, milk and mango pulp. It may be made with or without added sugar.

	--------------------------------------------------
	Lassi

Lassi, a creamy, frothy yogurt-based drink, blended with water and various fruits or seasonings (such as salt or sugar).
